How to Donate

Step 1: Contact Us

Reach out to us via phone or email to schedule a pickup or drop-off.

Step 2: Prepare Food

Package your surplus food items securely and label them clearly.

Step 3: Donate

We will collect the food from you or you can drop it off at our location.
